The dashboard visualizes key metrics from the dataset, including:

1. Average Tip by Day

A bar chart comparing tip amounts across weekdays to identify tipping trends.

2. Total Tip by Gender

A gender breakdown of total tips collected, helping uncover customer demographics and spending habits.

3. Average Total Bill KPI

A highlighted KPI card showing the overall average bill amount across all observations.

4. Average Total Bill by Day

A horizontal bar chart displaying daily spending patterns to determine high-revenue days.

5. Interactive Slicers

Users can filter charts by:

Time (Lunch vs Dinner)
This allows dynamic exploration of customer behavior.
